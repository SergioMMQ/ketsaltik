Why do we use body paint?

We use high-quality, hypoallergenic aqua color paints that are skin-friendly. These paints help us create long-lasting, vibrant and comfortable designs for any occasion.

They allow for a unique visual experience, combining textures and colors that enhance the body in artistic events, photo shoots and creative workshops.
